Welcome to Prediction Lab

This interactive tool will help you apply developmental psychology to children's media. Before you watch a clip or play a game, you will make theory-based predictions. Then, you will watch/play, gather evidence, and revise your claims.

Tab: Move forward to the next interactive element (input, button, link, checkbox).
Shift + Tab: Move backward to the previous element.
Spacebar: Select/deselect a checkbox, activate a button, or open a dropdown menu.
Enter: Activate a button, open a link, or confirm a dropdown selection.
Arrow Keys (Up/Down/Left/Right): Move between radio button options within a group, or scroll through a dropdown menu.
Escape (Esc): Close a dropdown menu without making a selection.

Choose the specific media clip or interactive game you have been assigned to analyze.

Your Goals

  • Predict: Make a theory-based claim about how children may respond to the media.
  • Test: Gather specific evidence from the clip/game to test your prediction.
  • Revise: Strengthen your claim into a developmental analysis.

Note: This tool runs entirely in your browser. No data is saved or collected. You will export your work as an image at the end.
